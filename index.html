Bio

Hi, I'm Haoyu Yue, a Ph.D. student at the Urban Design & Planning Interdisciplinary Ph.D. Program at the University of Washington, Seattle, USA. I'm also a part of the Urban Infrastructure Lab led by Prof. Jan Whittington at UW and the oversea city observer of Urban China Magazine. I have always had a passion about all stuff related to the city. Now my research interests include infrastructure planning, urban carbon governance, and the combination of urban studies and data science.
