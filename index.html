Bio

Hi, I'm Haoyu Yue, a master student at the department of Urban Design and Planning at the University of Washington, Seattle. I'm also a part of the Urban Infrastructure Lab led by Prof. Jan Whittington at UW and the oversea observer of Urban China Magazine. I have always had a passion about all stuff related to the city, such as rail system, skylines, the up-and-down stories of cities. Now my research interests include infrastructure planning, urban carbon governance, and urban studies under new technologies.
